Instruments in Classical Music

Violins and Other Strings

String instruments play an essential role in classical orchestras. Violins, violas, cellos, and double basses produce rich and expressive sounds.

Wind Instruments

Flutes, oboes, clarinets, and bassoons offer varied nuances and are often associated with lyrical melodies.

Piano and Harpsichord

The piano, an emblem of romantic music, and the harpsichord, a baroque icon, are two pillars of classical music.

Brass and Percussion

Trumpets, horns, trombones, and timpani bring power and brilliance to grand symphonies.
