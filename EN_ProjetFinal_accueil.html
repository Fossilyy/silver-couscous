"Classical music" is a generic term often used to refer to most music composed for orchestras from the Renaissance to the present day. However, music has evolved significantly between these two periods. Think about the evolution of music over the past 20 or 30 years; how could it not have evolved over the previous 500 years? Different eras were concerned with different themes, explored different styles, and created different sounds... How can all of that be reduced to just "classical music"? This website aims to teach the major musical movements of those 500 years and their unique characteristics.
