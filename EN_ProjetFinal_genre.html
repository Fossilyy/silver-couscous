Discover the Main Genres of Classical Music

Baroque

From 1600 to 1750, baroque music featured complex polyphonies and expressive compositions. Notable composers include Johann Sebastian Bach and Antonio Vivaldi.

Classical

The classical era (1750–1820) emphasized balance, clarity, and structure. Famous composers include Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart and Joseph Haydn.

Romantic

Between 1820 and 1900, romantic music prioritized emotion and individuality. Notable composers include Ludwig van Beethoven and Frédéric Chopin.

Modern

Starting in the 20th century, modern classical music explored innovation and experimentation. Prominent figures include Igor Stravinsky and Claude Debussy.
