International Baroque Music Festival

French music, which shone throughout Europe in the 17th and 18th centuries, gave birth to successive genres with bold forms that are a testament to this heritage: court air, court ballet, comédie-ballet, tragedy in music, opera-ballet, grand and small motet, opéra-comique… The names of Rameau, Charpentier, Lully, Bouzignac, Marais, Campra, Mondonville, Dauvergne, and Grétry, among many others, reflect the extraordinary artistic effervescence of this period.

VENUE AND DATE

Concerts are held every Thursday from November 2024 to April 2025, excluding holidays and vacations, at 5:30 PM, in the Royal Chapel of the Palace of Versailles.
Duration: approximately 1 hour.

TICKETS

Free entry, subject to availability (reservation recommended, mandatory for groups). Priority seating for members of the Rameau Circle of the CMBV.

Symphonic Concert: Beethoven and Brahms

Immerse yourself in an exceptional musical evening where the masterpieces of Beethoven and Brahms come to life under the baton of a renowned conductor. This concert will highlight the depth of Brahms' symphonies and the timeless intensity of Beethoven's compositions. A must-attend event for classical music lovers.

VENUE AND DATE

The concert will be held in the majestic Hall of Lights, renowned for its impeccable acoustics and elegant setting. Located in the heart of the city center, the hall features a ceiling adorned with frescoes and large balconies offering a panoramic view of the stage. A refined and warm atmosphere that will enhance every note played.

TICKETS

General admission: free
Access to the concert is offered free of charge, allowing everyone to enjoy this musical experience.
Open seating subject to availability.
It is recommended to arrive early to select a seat offering an optimal view of the orchestra.

Piano Masterclass

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of the piano during this exceptional masterclass. This unique event highlights talented performers, blending moments of sharing, musical performance, and learning. Whether you are a music lover, pianist, or simply curious, this evening promises a memorable musical experience.

VENUE AND DATE

The concert will take place in the elegant Saint-Philippe Auditorium, a warm and intimate space located in the heart of the city. Known for its exceptional acoustics and cozy ambiance, the venue provides an ideal setting to appreciate every nuance of piano performances.

TICKETS

Admission to this masterclass is completely free, but prior registration is recommended due to the limited number of seats.
Open seating, with doors opening at 7:30 PM for an 8:00 PM start. Arrive early to secure the best seats and fully enjoy the experience.

Opera Recital

Immerse yourself in the heart of the most beautiful works of the lyrical repertoire during this opera recital. The voices of the soloists, accompanied by a talented pianist, will transport you to a universe of emotions and virtuosity. An evening not to be missed by all classical music enthusiasts.

VENUE AND DATE

The concert will be held at the prestigious Saint-Eloi Theater, a gem of classical architecture located in the heart of the city. This intimate and warm venue, with its elegant woodwork and exceptional acoustics, offers an immersive experience for every spectator.

TICKETS

  • Ticket price: Free (reservation required).
  • Seating: Open seating, subject to availability. We recommend arriving early to choose the best seats.
